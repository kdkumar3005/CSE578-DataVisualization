Beyond Man's Best Friend

The soviet space dogs: This implementation uses multi level donut charts to depict the number of trips a dog has taken to space. The area of the arc tell us the altitude it reached in each flight. The color of the paw inserted in the center tell us if the dog survived it's last flight or not, Survived: Green, Died: Orange. The color of the arc depicts the gender of the dog, Blue: Male, Red: Female. The links between the two dogs show which two dogs flew together and in which rocket. The horizontal positioning shows the Year in which they travelled, ranging from 1951 to 1960.
